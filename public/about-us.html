About Us

Sonic Seats is a concert ticket-selling website started by Mario Solis and Evelyn Huerta in May 2024. We provide the best service for our customers in the DMV area!

Mario is a sophomore at Caltech majoring in Computer Science. Mario's favorite artists are Sebastian Yatra, The Weeknd, and Manuel Turizo.

Evelyn is also a sophomore at Caltech majoring in Computer Science with a secondary interest in Biology. Evelyn's favorite artists are Girl in Red, Noah Kahan, and Ariana Grande.

This website was created by Mario Solis collaboratively with Evelyn Huerta for the final project of CS 132 at Caltech in May-June 2024.
